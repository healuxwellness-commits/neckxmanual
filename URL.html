2. Safety Precautions

\ \

General Precautions

\
    \
  • Turn off the power when not in use
    • \
  • Do not immerse the product in water
    • \
  • If the massager gets wet, allow it to dry completely before use
    • \
  • Do not fold the graphene heating film located in the center of the massager
    • \
  • Let the device cool down before removing it from your neck
    • \
  • Avoid cold water on your neck right after use
    • \
\ \
\ Warning: Discontinue use and consult a doctor if discomfort occurs.\
\ \

Warnings

\
    \
  • Do not use with damaged cords or plugs
    • \
  • Do not disassemble the battery to prevent fire or electric shock
    • \
  • Avoid using if sensitive to heat or advised against heat compress therapy
    • \
  • Consult a medical professional before use if pregnant, have pacemakers, herniated discs, a history of stroke, or other serious medical conditions
    • \
  • Do not allow children under 10 or adults who are incapacitated to use this device
    • \

3. Instructions for Use

\ \

Charging the Device

\
    \
  • Use the Type-C adapter and connect to a 5V-1A power socket
    • \
  • Red Light: Charging
    • \
  • Green Light: Fully charged
    • \
\ \

Heat Function

\
    \
  • Long press the Heat button to turn on/off
    • \
  • High: Red | Medium: Blue | Low: Green
    • \
\ \

Vibration Function

\
    \
  • Long press the Vibration button to activate
    • \
  • Wave Mode: Red | Knock Mode: Blue | Rhythm Mode: Green
    • \
\ \

Care & Storage

\
    \
  • Cool before storage: let the device cool before putting it away
    • \
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight or heat sources
    • \
  • Cleaning: wipe gently with a soft, dry cloth (avoid damp cloths and volatile liquids like gasoline or alcohol)
