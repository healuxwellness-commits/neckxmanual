3. Safety Precautions

General Precautions

  • Turn off the power when not in use
  • Do not immerse the product in water
  • If the massager gets wet, allow it to dry completely before use
  • Do not fold the graphene heating film in the center of the massager
  • Let the device cool before removing it from your neck
  • Avoid cold water on the neck immediately after use
Warnings:
  • Do not use with damaged cords or plugs
  • Discontinue use if discomfort occurs and consult a doctor
  • Do not disassemble the battery
  • Avoid use if sensitive to heat therapy
  • Consult a doctor before use if pregnant or with medical conditions
  • Not suitable for children under 10 or incapacitated adults

4. Instructions for Use

Charging the Device

  • Use a Type-C adapter with a 5V-1A power socket
  • Red light: Charging
  • Green light: Fully charged

Heat Function

  • Long press the Heat button to turn on/off
  • High: Red | Medium: Blue | Low: Green

Vibration Function

  • Long press the Vibration button to activate
  • Wave: Red | Knock: Blue | Rhythm: Green

Care & Storage

  • Allow the device to cool before storage
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth only
